BioRocket Internship

Our BioRocket program consists of a spring after-school hands-on science program and a summer research internship experience for New York City public and charter high school students. This a great opportunity for students to gain real-world experience in the life sciences, and to design and implement a team research project

Category: Science

Grade: 9-12

Location: Brooklyn, NY

Human Oncology & Pathogenesis Program (HOPP)

Students will have a chance to take part in an 8-week, biomedical or computational lab-based internship in a Memorial Sloan Kettering (MSK) research lab. Students are paired with a mentor who supervises their activities, helping them develop appropriate technical skills. Over the course of the internship, students complete a self-directed project that supports the Principal Investigators overall research objectives. Through participation in laboratory meetings and program sessions, students are exposed to a variety of people within their assigned laboratories and MSK community

Location: Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, New York, NY

Category: Science

Grade: 9-12

The Cooper Union STEM Program

A prestigious program held in the East Village. It offers college-level engineering and computer science classes taught in Cooper Union's facilities. Financial aid is available, with priority for NYC public school students. Applications for Summer 2026 are currently closed, but planning for 2027 should start in December 2026.

Location: Manhattan, NY

Category: Engineering

Grade: 9-12

Cost: $3,150 (3-week) / $5,150 (6-week) - financial aid available, priority for NYC public school students

Deadline: Applications for Summer 2026 are currently closed - plan for December 2026 (2027 cycle)

NYC FIRST STEM Centers

Physical robotics hubs offering free after-school and weekend open hours. Students can drop in to use 3D printers, laser cutters, soldering stations, and robotics components with expert staff on hand. Some locations also offer credit-bearing high school robotics courses. Great for FIRST Robotics team members or anyone wanting to build.

Location: Cornell Tech (Roosevelt Island), Washington Heights (Manhattan), South Bronx, Far Rockaway (Queens)

Category: Robotics, Engineering,

Grade: K-12

Cost: Free

Deadline: Rolling-Open year round

Organization: NYC FIRST
