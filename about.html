About STEMAtlas

STEMAtlas is a platform designed to help middle and high school students discover STEM opportunities, including competitions, internships, research programs, scholarships, volunteer opportunities, and learning resources.

Why STEMAtlas Was Created

Finding STEM opportunities can be challenging. There are many valuable programs, competitions, and internships are spread across different websites, and this wasting and making it difficult for students to discover opportunities that match their interests.

STEMAtlas was created to bring these opportunities together in one organized platform, making STEM education more accessible for students.

Founder

STEMAtlas was founded by a student who wanted to make STEM opportunities easier for students to discover and access.

Future Development

Future versions of STEMAtlas may include student accounts, personalized recommendations, opportunity tracking, community partnerships, and additional STEM learning tools.
